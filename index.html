Researches and Activities

2018.08  

A poster has been demonstrated in Innovation in Construction Forum 2018, U of Alberta:

Calculating Dirt-Loading Cycles by Deep Learning Detector and Kalman Filter


Earth moving work is one of the most important activities in construction sites. It is time and labor consuming to estimate productivity manually during earth moving operations. In this research, a hybrid method has been proposed to detect and track construction equipment automatically from video stream in order to calculate dirt-loading cycles.
